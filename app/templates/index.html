        About Nano
        
        
      
        
      
        
        
        
          n
          
        
            
        Nano
        
            
        Your document intelligence assistant
        
          
        
        
        
        
        Nano is a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) assistant that lets you upload documents and ask questions in natural language. Answers are grounded in your own files — not generic web knowledge.
        
        
        What Nano can do
        
        
          
          
        • Upload PDF, DOCX, TXT, CSV, and XLSX files
          • 
          
        • Index and search your content semantically
          • 
          
        • Answer questions using the most relevant passages from your documents
          • 
          
        • Download and manage indexed files
          • 
        
        
        
        How it works
        
        
        When you upload a file, Nano extracts the text, splits it into searchable chunks, and stores them in a vector database. When you ask a question, Nano finds the best matching chunks and uses them to generate a focused answer.
        
        
        Built for teams who need fast, accurate answers from their own knowledge base.
